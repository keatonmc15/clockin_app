Privacy Policy

ClockIn – Employee Timekeeping Application
Effective Date: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction

ClockIn (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is operated by C&C Supply and Services CO. INC. and provides a mobile employee timekeeping application (“App”) designed for workforce attendance tracking and payroll support.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed, and safeguarded when authorized employees use the App.

ClockIn is not a consumer-facing application. It is made available exclusively to employees of businesses that have authorized its use (each, an “Employer”).

By using the App, users acknowledge and consent to the collection and processing of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Role of the Employer

The Employer controls employee accounts and determines how timekeeping data is used within its organization.

ClockIn processes employee information on behalf of the Employer for legitimate employment-related and business purposes.

Employees should direct questions regarding time records, corrections, or data removal requests to their Employer.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Employment and Account Information

We collect information provided by the Employer, including:

  • Employee name
  • Employee identification number
  • Assigned store or job location
  • Login credentials or PIN
  • Employment status

This information is necessary to authenticate users and associate time entries with the appropriate employee account.

3.2 Location Information

ClockIn collects precise GPS location data during active work shifts.

Location data may be collected:

  • When an employee clocks in
  • While an employee remains clocked in
  • During background operation while a shift is active
  • When entering or exiting designated geofence boundaries
  • When clocking out

Background location is accessed only during an active work shift and automatically stops once the shift ends.

Location data is used solely to:

  • Verify employee presence at assigned work sites
  • Verify employee presence at assigned work locations
  • Prevent inaccurate time entries
  • Automatically detect shift exits
  • Maintain payroll accuracy

ClockIn does not track employees outside of active work shifts. ClockIn does not use location data for marketing, advertising, profiling, or resale.

3.3 Technical and Device Information

We may collect limited technical information necessary for system operation, including:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Location permission status
  • Timestamped event logs

This information is used strictly for application security, diagnostics, fraud prevention, and system integrity.

4. Purpose of Processing

Information is processed for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Recording employee work hours
  • Payroll reporting and calculations
  • Compliance with labor and employment regulations
  • Dispute resolution and audit documentation
  • Maintaining system security and reliability

We do not use collected information for advertising, behavioral tracking, or unrelated commercial purposes.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Employee information may be disclosed only to:

  • The Employer and authorized administrative personnel
  • Payroll processors or accounting personnel designated by the Employer
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers necessary to operate the service

We do not:

  • Sell personal information
  • Share personal information for behavioral advertising
  • Share personal information for cross-app tracking
  • Provide employee data to third-party marketing platforms

6. Data Retention

Employee data is retained:

  • For the duration of employment
  • As required for payroll, tax, or labor law compliance
  • As necessary to resolve disputes or enforce agreements

Data may be deleted upon request by the Employer, subject to applicable legal obligations.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards designed to protect personal information, including:

  • Encrypted transmission via HTTPS
  • Secure cloud hosting infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication protections
  • Role-based administrative permissions

While we use commercially reasonable safeguards, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Background Location Disclosure

The App collects background location data during active work shifts to:

  • Confirm employee presence at assigned work sites
  • Detect geofence entry and exit
  • Automatically close shifts when an employee leaves a designated area

Background location tracking:

  • Is active only during a clocked-in shift
  • Stops when the shift ends
  • Is not used for surveillance outside work hours

Employees must grant appropriate location permissions for the App to function as intended.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be reflected by an updated “Effective Date.” Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
